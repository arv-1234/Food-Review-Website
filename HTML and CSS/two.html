Top 5 Best Resteraunts in Toronto

1. Alo Restaurant

When Alo opened in 2015 with its high-concept French cuisine, it didn’t take long for it to top critic’s choice lists around the world. The naysayers eventually became believers. The space is elegant and filled with natural light. There is also an adequate amount of room to move around. Anybody willing to commit to a five-course is bound to enjoy everything the restaurant has to offer. The cocktails available include house mixtures and the classics and favourites. Alo has some of the best wines in Canada, with pricey and approachable bottles. The menu takes ingredients and flavours from all over the world and adds a classic French twist to it. Also, the menu changes with the seasons so you will have encounter different foods at different times.

2. Chubby's Jamaican

Chubbys is for those who enjoy eating jerk with their hands while drinking some signature house cocktails. The drinks they serve are almost as good as the jerk. The tropical versions of classic cocktails are loved by many. If you are in a large group try out some of the jumbo options like the jumbo pina colada. This is the best place for Jamaican food in Toronto. There are great burgers and salty fish fritters, but the main course is the jerk chicken, which takes about four days to prepare. Make room for dessert, the creamy goodness provides a nice balance to the jerk’s heat. So whenever you get a craving for spicy food, especially during the winter, you know where to go.

3. Canoe Restaurant

Canoe has the best view in Toronto, the CN Tower on one side and the planes landing at the airport on the other. The food is just as good as where the restaurant is located. If you want to celebrate a special occasion this is the place to go. The menu has an incredible blend of flavours and ingredients from all across Canada. You will find fine Ontario produce and foie gras from Quebec. Their service is exceptional. The glasses are always filled and your cutlery will be replaced quickly. When you have to make a big impression or you just have money to blow Canoe is the place to do it.

4. The Jerk Box

The Jerk Box serves delicious Jamaican food for breakfast, lunch and dinner including some tasty desserts. Customers line up at this restaurant for the mouthwatering food served from a hot plate. The types of soup and porridge that change throughout the week and starting 7 am breakfast is available. Most of the items available for lunch include chicken dressed in spices, bringing out the jerk flavouring. The classic jerk chicken is juicy and flavourful. You can eat it in many different ways like getting it fried, BBQ’d or even as a stew. Other than the hot meals, there is a great selection of desserts such as rum cake, cherry sponge cake and velvet cake. This place is a go-to spot for many in the area so why not take a chance? And try it out.

5. Cabano's Comfort Food

Cabanos comfort food is phenomenal. It easily has the best chicken sandwich in the GTA. Great customer service and an especially clean environment (very important during the covid pandemic!). Definitely in the higher price range in terms of comfort food, although the price is justified. The chicken sandwich was very well made with a perfect contrast between the soft bun and crispy fried chicken as well as the rich Cabano sauce and tangy pickles. The chicken wings tossed with the burnt chile sauce are my favourite out of all the other signature house-made sauces. This is a must-eat place! So make sure to check it out if you have the chance.
